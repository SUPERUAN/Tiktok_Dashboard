Cloud Service

Cloud Service by Infomeis is a lightweight cloud-based analytics and reporting tool designed to help creators, agencies, and internal marketing teams organize TikTok performance data in one place. The product connects to approved APIs, collects video-level metrics such as views, likes, comments, shares, and engagement-related performance data, and presents them in a simple dashboard for review and export.

The service is intended for internal reporting, operational tracking, and business analysis. It can be used to review account performance, monitor individual content results, and export selected data into CSV or spreadsheet-friendly formats for downstream reporting.
